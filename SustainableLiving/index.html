Daily Actions

Daily Sustainable Living Tips

Small changes in your daily routine can make a big difference. Here are practical tips to get started.

üè† At Home

  • Unplug electronics when not in use
  • Use LED bulbs and energy-efficient appliances
  • Take shorter showers and fix leaky faucets
  • Plant trees and create green spaces
  • Use reusable bags, bottles, and containers

üõí Shopping

  • Choose products with minimal packaging
  • Buy local and seasonal produce
  • Opt for durable, high-quality items
  • Avoid single-use plastics
  • Support eco-friendly brands

üöó Transportation

  • Use public transportation or carpool
  • Walk, bike, or use electric scooters
  • Maintain proper tire pressure for fuel efficiency
  • Combine errands to reduce trips
  • Consider electric or hybrid vehicles
