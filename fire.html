Ways to be Prepared

Fire Preparedness Strategies

Prevention Measures

  • Install smoke detectors on every floor and in bedrooms
  • Test smoke detectors monthly and replace batteries annually
  • Keep flammable materials away from heat sources
  • Never leave cooking, candles, or heaters unattended
  • Maintain electrical systems and avoid overloading circuits
  • Store matches and lighters safely away from children

Emergency Planning

  • Create a detailed fire escape plan with two routes per room
  • Practice fire drills with family every 6 months
  • Designate a safe meeting point outside your home
  • Teach children how to call 911 and what to say
  • Keep fire extinguishers accessible and know how to use them
  • Plan for pets and elderly family members

Safety Equipment

  • Install ABC-type fire extinguishers in kitchen and garage
  • Consider fire sprinkler systems for added protection
  • Keep fire blankets in kitchen for grease fires
  • Maintain fire-resistant doors and windows
  • Have emergency ladders for upper floors
  • Store important documents in fireproof containers

Ways to Mitigate Accidents

Fire Accident Mitigation Strategies

Structural & Technical

  • Install automatic fire suppression systems (sprinklers)
  • Use fire-resistant building materials
  • Install fire doors and fire-resistant windows
  • Maintain proper electrical systems with circuit breakers
  • Install smoke and heat detection systems
  • Keep fire extinguishers in accessible locations

Behavioral & Policy

  • Enforce no-smoking policies in high-risk areas
  • Conduct regular fire drills and training
  • Establish clear evacuation procedures
  • Maintain clear exit paths and emergency lighting
  • Regular inspection and maintenance of fire safety equipment
  • Community fire safety education programs
