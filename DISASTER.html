Ways to Be Prepared

Preparedness Playbook

Anticipate

  • Use GeoRiskPH and NOAH to layer hazards with assets.
  • Run worst-case scenario planning every quarter.
  • Create redundancy: power, comms, supply, leadership.

Absorb

  • Invest in resilient infrastructure (green roofs, permeable pavements).
  • Adopt modular shelters, floating storage for floods.
  • Strengthen social protection (cash-for-work, microinsurance).

Adapt

  • Conduct after-action reviews within 72 hours post-event.
  • Update policies based on community-led data.
  • Integrate mental health first aid into recovery.

Mitigation Strategies

Ways to Mitigate Accidents & Secondary Crises

Structural & Technological

  • Install automatic shutoff valves (gas/water) triggered by sensors.
  • Adopt smart meters + thermal imaging for early fire detection.
  • Retrofit bridges/buildings with base isolators and flood vents.

Behavioral & Policy

  • Institute zero-burn policies, enforce no-build zones.
  • Mandate quarterly integrated drills (earthquake + fire + evacuation).
  • Provide micro-grants for community risk reduction innovations.
