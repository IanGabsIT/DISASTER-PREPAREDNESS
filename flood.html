Ways to be Prepared

Flood Preparedness Strategies

Before a Flood

  • Know your area's flood risk and evacuation routes
  • Elevate electrical panels, water heaters, and appliances
  • Install check valves to prevent backflow
  • Clear gutters, drains, and drainage systems
  • Prepare emergency kit with 72-hour supplies
  • Store important documents in waterproof containers

During a Flood

  • Move to higher ground immediately
  • Never walk or drive through floodwaters
  • Evacuate if authorities recommend
  • Turn off utilities at main switches if safe
  • Stay away from downed power lines
  • Listen to battery-powered radio for updates

After a Flood

  • Return home only when authorities say it's safe
  • Avoid floodwaters - may be contaminated
  • Inspect utilities before turning on
  • Clean and disinfect everything that got wet
  • Document damage for insurance claims
  • Watch for health issues from contaminated water

Ways to Mitigate Accidents

Flood Accident Mitigation Strategies

Structural & Technical

  • Build flood barriers and levees
  • Improve drainage systems and infrastructure
  • Install flood gates and pumps
  • Elevate buildings and critical infrastructure
  • Create retention ponds and green spaces
  • Implement early warning systems

Behavioral & Policy

  • Enforce no-build zones in floodplains
  • Implement proper waste management
  • Maintain and clear drainage systems regularly
  • Community flood preparedness programs
  • Land use planning and zoning regulations
  • Public education on flood risks
